About Sunny

With 18 years of tattooing experience, Sunny has established himself as one of the premier tattoo artists in the Coachella Valley and Yucaipa, CA area. Specializing in geometric, mandala, dotwork, black & grey realism, and ornamental tattoos, Sunny's work represents a perfect fusion of precision and artistry.

In 2021, Sunny was honored with the "Best in Coachella Valley" award, recognizing his exceptional skill and dedication to the craft. Beyond his client work, Sunny is also the proud owner of Sacred Black Ink, a tattoo supply brand that reflects his commitment to quality and the sacred nature of the tattoo experience.

As part of the Sullen family and Pro team, Sunny continues to push the boundaries of geometric and sacred geometry tattoo artistry. His YouTube channel and social media presence showcase not only his finished work but also his process, inspiring both clients and fellow artists worldwide.
