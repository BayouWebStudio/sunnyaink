Example Requests

Here's how to write good change requests

✅

Good Request

Type: Text Update

Description: "On the About section of the homepage, please change 'I've been tattooing for 5 years' to 'I've been tattooing for 6 years'"

Why it's good: Specific location, exact current text, exact new text.

❌

Unclear Request

Type: Text Update

Description: "Update my bio"

Why it's unclear: Which bio? What page? Change to what?

✅

Good Request

Type: Image Change

Description: "Replace the 3rd image in the gallery (the rose tattoo) with the attached dragon piece. Title should be 'Dragon Back Piece - Fresh'"

Why it's good: Specific location, clear replacement, includes new image and title.

✅

Good Request

Type: Price Update

Description: "In the Services section, change 'Small tattoos: $120 minimum' to 'Small tattoos: $150 minimum'"

Why it's good: Exact section, exact current pricing, exact new pricing.
